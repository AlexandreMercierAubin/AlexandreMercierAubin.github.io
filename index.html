Biography

I am a PhD student of McGill University and supervised by Paul G. Kry. My research subject is computer graphics and algorithm design. I completed my MSc on constraint programming in 2020 at Université Laval, supervised by Claude-Guy Quimper and Jonathan Gaudreault.
